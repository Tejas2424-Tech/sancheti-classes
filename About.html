About Sancheti Classes

At Sancheti, students are inspired to perform and grow — get to know our story, our founder, and what drives us.

FOUNDER & MD
Dipesh Sancheti

Since 2000, Sancheti Classes has grown with the trust and support of students, parents, and well-wishers. Over the years we have kept pace with changing educational patterns, building experienced faculty and an open, highly interactive environment between teachers and students.

We believe in inspirational guidance that builds interpersonal skills and healthy competition, helping every student stay motivated toward their goals — whether that means competitive exams or board-level success.

"Each student carries nuclear potential — we only guide that energy toward its target."

— Dipesh Sancheti, Founder & MD

Founder's Message

Teaching since 2000 has shown us that students scoring between 65% and 85% often carry the same underlying potential — what sets top scorers apart is how they apply what they've learned through consistent practice. That's why we focus special effort on building interpersonal skills through extensive question-paper practice.

Our team has built dedicated academic programs around this idea. We start by strengthening core fundamentals — especially in language, science, and mathematics — with clear explanations and plenty of examples. Once those fundamentals are solid, we shift focus toward competitive-exam readiness, since Class 9 lays the foundation for Classes 11 and 12. For Class 10 specifically, we've developed dedicated, highly competitive notes and extensive practice material drawn from years of experience.

Dipesh Sancheti
